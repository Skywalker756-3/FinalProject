Understanding Lunar Phases

The moon moves through various phases, from the New Moon to the Full Moon. Each phase carries unique energy that influences emotions and growth. Understanding these phases helps align with the cosmic flow of energy.

The Moon and Zodiac Signs

Each moon phase interacts uniquely with zodiac signs. The Full Moon energizes fire signs (Aries, Leo, Sagittarius), while earth signs (Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn) feel most connected during the Waxing Crescent.

Use lunar energy to set intentions during the New Moon, celebrate achievements under the Full Moon, and let go of negativity during the Waning Moon. Align your actions with the moon to enhance personal growth.
